Your grid

Each pair works both ways: the field you edited last (solid border) governs, the other (dashed) shows the result. A count sets the total cm; a total cm computes the count as ⌊(total − 1,8) / (opening + 1,8)⌋ with spare cm reported — spare height of ≥ 1 cm usable opening becomes a shorter top section with 0–5 evenly spaced dividers, and spare width becomes a narrow full-height side column with 0–5 evenly spaced shelves (the "divisions" inputs above). Construction: top and bottom boards run the full outer width; the sides stand on the bottom board, sandwiched under the top for rigidity; middle shelves fit between the sides; short dividers fill each row. Mitred corners run both the caps and the sides to the full outer size (long point of the 45° cut); the dado inset lengthens every internal board by twice the inset so it seats into grooves — outer dimensions and openings are unchanged. Board thickness follows the chosen depth (1,8 cm for most widths; a few only exist in 1,5 or 2,7 cm, noted in the dropdown); the shopping plan reserves a 3 mm saw kerf per cut. IKEA's Kallax opening is ≈33×33 cm at 39 cm depth, so 40 cm boards are the closest match.
